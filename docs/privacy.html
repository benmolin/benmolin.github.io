SNAPscreener.com Privacy Policy and Terms of Use

Terms of Use

Background

The mission of this website and SNAP prescreener is to help connect individuals to SNAP benefits and to make SNAP eligibility data more accessible. This project is open source, meaning that anybody can see the code used to power it, collaborate on the project, and re-use the project however they'd like.

License

See the full license of this project.

Accuracy Disclaimer

All estimates and data on this website are for educational purposes only and provide a basic estimate based on publicly available information. State test, benefit, and allowance amounts may be incomplete, incorrect, or outdated due to limitations in finding updated sources. Eligibility factors vary between states, and some deductions and eligibility criteria will not be included here. The only way to find out your true eligibility and benefit amount is to apply.

Unofficial Disclaimer

This website is not affiliated with any government organization or SNAP program. Please refer to your state's or the USDA's website official guidance on SNAP eligibility.



Privacy Policy

Background

We use third party technology to collect information about how our visitors use our website. We are not interested in the identity of any individuals that visit our website to use the prescreener, but we do collect some personal information about visitors (like IP address) that could be used to identify an individual. We use technology to see what visitors are doing on our website so that we can improve our website. We also collect some screener use and pageview data that we will share with our partners so that they can see how many people they were able to assist using our website.

This website uses GoatCounter to monitor pages views and LogRocket to capture and playback session recordings. We may share all the data collected by GoatCounter publicly. This website is hosted on GitHub pages and uses CloudFlare to make the website load faster, provide SSL, and provide basic traffic statistics.

What This Policy Covers

This Privacy Policy applies to information that SnapScreener.com ("SnapScreener.com," "this website", "we," "us" or "our") collects when you use our Services here on this website, SnapScreener.com.

Information We Collect

GoatCounter

This website uses GoatCounter to measure the number of visitors to this website, what pages were visited, and the number of successful "screens."

The following attributes are collected by GoatCounter:

  • Page URL (and parameters)
  • Referer header
  • User-Agent header
  • Screen size
  • Country name based on IP address
  • A hash of the IP address, User-Agent, and random number

We also use GoatCounter to collect information on estimated eligibility and estimated benefit amount.

LogRocket

This website uses LogRocket to collect information about how users use our website, and collects session logs in order to learn how we can improve the website and/or fix any errors. Logrocket collects information including but not limited to all mouse movements, eligibility information entered, pages viewed, and buttons clicked. LogRocket collects basic device information, such as browser, device, IP address and ISP information. LogRocket also sets cookies, trackers, and collects cookie information in order to allow us to identify repeat vistors to our website.

Please note that LogRocket states: "These cookies do not track the same user across unrelated domains and, as described above, attempts to infer identity across domains is strictly prohibited."

Please see the LogRocket privacy policy for more details.

CloudFlare

This website uses CloudFlare to make the website load faster, provide SSL, and provide basic traffic analytics. CloudFlare does set a cookie to prevent malicious traffic.

How We Share Information

We may make our website statistics from GoatCounter public or share them with partners, for example but not limited to: how many times the screener was used by state, what percentage of individuals were eligible, typical benefit amounts, how many times different pages were viewed, and which websites are referring visitors to our site. The statistics we may share would not be able to be used to identify an individual.

We do not share personal information from LogRocket outside of our team.
